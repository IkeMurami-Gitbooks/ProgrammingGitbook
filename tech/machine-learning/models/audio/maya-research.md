# Maya Research

Maya Research is a research lab building Voice Intelligence. We are creating models that can reason in speech and talk back like humans do. Most of our work is open source.

sites:

* [https://huggingface.co/maya-research](https://huggingface.co/maya-research)
* [https://www.mayaresearch.ai/](https://www.mayaresearch.ai/)

2 модели:

* maya1
  * Разные языки, может эмоции добавлять
* Veena: text-to-speech
  * Hindi, English
